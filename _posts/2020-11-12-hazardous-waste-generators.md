The HazWaste database contains generator (companies and/or individuals) site and mailing address information, waste generation, the amount of waste generated etc. of all the hazardous waste generators in Vermont. Database was developed in early 1990's for program management and to meet  EPA Authorization requirements.  The database has been updated to more modern data systems periodically.ï¿½
